A little about me

My name is Mariana and I was born in 1999. I grew up always connected to the arts and recently discovered the connection it can have with technology. My goal is to make the most of both areas, exploring concepts such as motion design and web design.

I am from Portugal and grew up in a small town in Aveiro, Águeda. I currently live in Coimbra, the city of students where I took my degree in Design & Multimedia at the Faculty of Science and Technology at the University of Coimbra.
